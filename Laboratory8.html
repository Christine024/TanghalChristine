Internet the World Wide Web

Many peopple use the terms Internet and the World Wide Web interchangeably, but in the first the two terms are not synonymous. The internet and the Web are two separated but related things.

The Internet is a massive network of networks, a networking infrastructure. it connects milions of computers together globally, forming a network in which any computer can communicate with other computers as long as they are both connected to the internet. Information can travel via the internet via a variety of languages called "Protocols"

The World Wide Web or simply "Web" is a way of accessing information over the medium of the internet. The web users the HTTP protocol, only of the languages spoken over the internet, to transmit data
